Story
The Naish Family has been growing riesling, chardonnay and pinot noir grapes at Black Estate since 1994. The terroir in this corner of the Waipara Valley has been informed by dense limestone and clay-limestone soils and a climate marked by warm days and cool nights. The family takes a hands on approach to viticulture and their gentleness and attention to detail in the winemaking process become evident in a superior product.
